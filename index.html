Product

Ideal for gardening and agriculture, coco peat promotes healthy root growth and reduces the need for chemical fertilizers!

Coco peat, also known as coir pith or coco coir, is a natural byproduct of coconut husk processing. It is widely used in gardening and agriculture as a sustainable and eco-friendly growing medium. Coco peat is renowned for its excellent water retention, aeration, and nutrient-holding capacity, making it an ideal choice for soil improvement, hydroponics, and seed starting. This versatile material is also biodegradable and promotes healthy root growth, reducing the need for chemical fertilizers.
